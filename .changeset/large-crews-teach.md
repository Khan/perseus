Render visible point labels on interactive graphs when the `perseus-enable-point-label-field` flag is on and the graph's `showPointLabels` field is true. Labels render as plain text in the Symbola font and stay outside the plotted region while a point is dragged. Supported on all interactive graph types except `vector` and `none`.
